A reminder

It is all too easy to feel that cooking is a burden, a distraction, and a chore. For that reason I've chosen to put this quote at the top of my recipe website. It serves as a reminder every time I pull it up to cook that I am about to partake in an ancient, life-sustaining ritual and to find joy in the mundane routines that life is full of.

"One of the very nicest things about life is the way we must regularly stop whatever it is we are doing and devote our attention to eating."

-Luciano Pavarotti
